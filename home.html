What is this website about?

On this website, you can learn all about the internet, and its different components, covering many topics, as well as a dedicated timeline filled with internet historical events.

There are multiple different topics that you can look at, by hovering over the definitions, and some have a special demo, showing how they work. The website allows you to look at a structured timeline, highlighting key events in internet history.

Who is this website for?

This is for people who are curious about how the internet works, or want to study the internet and its features. So if that's you, then welcome aboard!

On this website, you can find many indepth definitions and try features of the definitions. Soon, maybe you can become an expert programmer!
